About Zawia Academy

Building Future Leaders Through Quality Education Since 2009

Our Story

Zawia Academy was established in 2009 with a vision to provide world-class education to students in Islamabad. What started as a small initiative has now grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Over the years, we have consistently produced top-performing students who have excelled not just academically, but also in character development and moral values. Our commitment to excellence has made us the preferred choice for parents seeking quality education for their children.

Today, with two campuses and a team of highly qualified educators, we continue our mission of nurturing young minds and preparing them for a successful future.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential through innovative teaching methodologies, modern facilities, and a nurturing environment. We strive to develop critical thinking, creativity, and moral values in every student.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Islamabad, recognized for academic excellence, character building, and producing responsible citizens who contribute positively to society. We envision a future where our students become leaders in their chosen fields.
